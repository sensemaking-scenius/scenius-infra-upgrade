New TG community (infra)structure: main group + opt-in subgroups + Drip channel.

**Source:** [Notion proposal](https://m4co.notion.site/Telegram-Group-Restructuring-Proposal-31496ae9065580a69dcddf02cd483a45)

## Background & Opportunity Space

As the Scenius Telegram community has grown, so has the volume and variety of conversation, creating compounding issues:

- **Information overload.** Members who step away return to a wall of messages across wildly different topics: links, project updates, philosophical threads, logistics, and memes all tangled together.
- **Low signal-to-noise.** Some members want deep dives on tools and methodology. Others want community news and events. Everyone gets everything, so it feels noisy for most.
- **No clear home for subgroup activity.** Interest clusters form organically but lack dedicated space. Energy gets diffused or lost.

## The Proposal

**Principle:** Simplify what everyone sees. Expand what people can opt into. Keep a synthesis layer so nothing important gets lost.

### Part 1: Simplify the Main Supergroup

Reduce the main Telegram supergroup to core topics:

| Topic | Purpose |
|-------|---------|
| General Chat | Open conversation, relationship-building, community culture, new member onboarding |
| Links & Resources | Shared articles, tools, and references — no discussion required |
| Events & Bulletin Board | Zoom meetings, offline meetups, announcements, community logistics |
| Governance | Proposals, votes, and open discussions on Scenius stewardship |
| Sensemaking Drip | Synthesized digest of community activity (read-only rollup) across subgroups and sync meetings |

Everything else moves into subgroups. The main space becomes a calmer, more navigable home base.

### Part 2: Opt-In Interest Subgroups

Members join 2-5 subgroups based on interests. Each subgroup:

- Is a separate Telegram group (not just a topic/thread)
- Has a volunteer steward for light facilitation
- Feeds into summarized digests for broader community awareness

**Suggested subgroups:** Scenius Builders, AI tools and workshops, Sensemaking-as-a-Service, Fundraising support, Events (own + external), Scenius Bildung, Psychedelics, Politics, Religion.

Subgroups can form organically — they don't need to be perfect at launch. The lifecycle process (proposing to sunsetting) gets designed later.

### Part 3: The Drip as Synthesis Layer

The [Sensemaking Drip](http://t.me/scenius) channel serves as connective tissue:

- Auto-generated summaries of activity across channels, subgroups, and sync meetings via [scenius-digest](http://github.com/sensemaking-scenius/scenius-digest)
- Members stay up-to-date without reading every conversation
- Reduces FOMO for people who can't follow everything in real time
- Non-members can learn from the community and apply to join
- Future integration with scenius.space and Bluesky

**Desired outcome:** subgroup depth doesn't come at the cost of community coherence.

## Privacy & Archiving Baseline

- **Internal archiving:** open — implicit consent when joining the community
- **Public-facing content:** private by default, de-identified, with an opt-in review period before publication (modeled on a 48-hour review window)

This protects members while enabling the community to build a meaningful knowledge commons over time.
